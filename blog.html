Life on Mars

28/07/2017

It takes at least 2,000 people to create a "genetic pool" for a community that can survive on Mars. And assuming that the radiation will not sterilize them. But people born on Mars will be from a whole new world and a culture that is not similar to that on Earth. In this way, people would solve the mystery of "Martians" by becoming themselves - Martians ...
